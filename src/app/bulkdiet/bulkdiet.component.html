Diet basics

Food choices in the slimming diet depend on the number of calories that should be consumed per day,
when following a low-calorie drying regimen, you must maintain 30% to 40% of protein,
with about 30% to 50% of calories from carbohydrates, along with 20% of healthy fats.

Allowed foods:

Meat, fish, eggs, vegetables, fruits and nuts.

Not allowed foods:

Sugar and carbohydrate sources such as rice, pasta, wheat (white bread), seed oils, trans fats, fast food,
and soft drinks.
