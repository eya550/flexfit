Diet for Bulking

When aiming to bulk up, it's essential to consume a surplus of calories to support muscle growth and development. This typically involves a higher intake of protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats.

Recommended macronutrient distribution:

Protein: 25-35% of total calories

Carbohydrates: 45-65% of total calories

Fats: 20-35% of total calories

Allowed foods:

Lean meats (chicken breast, turkey, lean beef), fish, eggs, dairy products (milk, yogurt), whole grains (brown rice, quinoa, oats), legumes (beans, lentils), fruits, vegetables, nuts, seeds, healthy oils (olive oil, avocado oil).

Not recommended:

Limit processed foods, sugary snacks, and excessive intake of saturated and trans fats. However, small amounts of these foods can still be included occasionally to meet calorie goals.
